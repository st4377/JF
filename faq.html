FAQ

What is Jagathi Foundation?+
Jagathi Foundation is a registered non-profit organization established in 2020, dedicated to empowering underserved communities through education, health awareness, women empowerment, environmental action, and sustainable livelihoods.
Where does Jagathi Foundation operate?+
We currently work across eight Indian states, including Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Puducherry, Himachal Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra and others.
How can I volunteer with Jagathi Foundation?+
You can join our initiatives by signing up through our Join Us page. Opportunities include education programs, health camps, environmental drives, and women empowerment initiatives.
How can I donate to Jagathi Foundation?+
Individuals and corporates can donate through bank transfer or online via our Get Involved Donations support our programs in education, health, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.
Can corporates partner with Jagathi Foundation?+
Yes! We collaborate with corporates through CSR partnerships, employee engagement programs, and project sponsorships. Contact us for details at Contact Us.
How are donations used?+
All donations are directly used for program implementation, including education support, medical camps, livelihood initiatives, environmental activities, and community outreach. Transparency reports are available on request.
How can I stay updated on Jagathi Foundation’s activities?+
You can follow our News & Updates section, subscribe to newsletters, and follow our social media handles.
Does Jagathi Foundation accept in-kind donations?+
Yes, we accept contributions like school kits, clothing, food items, and other essentials. Please contact us for specific requirements and logistics.
How can I organize a fundraising event for Jagathi Foundation?+
Individuals or groups interested in fundraising can reach out through our Contact Us page for guidance and support.
Is Jagathi Foundation a registered NGO eligible for tax benefits?+
Yes, Jagathi Foundation is a registered NGO, and donations may qualify for tax deductions under applicable laws. Receipts are provided for all contributions.
