Who We Are

A Worldwide Non-Profit Charity

Jagathi Foundation is a registered non-profit organization founded on 8th October 2020 with a mission to empower underserved communities through education, health awareness, women empowerment, environmental action, and sustainable livelihoods.

We operate across eight states in India, focusing on grassroots change and long-term impact. Working closely with volunteers, corporates, government bodies, and partner institutions, we implement impactful programs that uplift communities and inspire social responsibility.
