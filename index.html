Who We Are

Largest global crowdfunding community

Jagathi Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization committed to uplifting underprivileged and vulnerable communities across India. Since its inception on 8th October 2020, the foundation has been actively working across eight states, driving impactful initiatives in:

  • Education & Skill Development
  • Health Awareness & Wellness Campaigns
  • Women Empowerment & Livelihood Support
  • Environmental Sustainability & Climate Action
  • Community Development & Social Welfare

What We Do

Jagathi Foundation collaborates

With a mission to build an equitable, eco-conscious, and empowered society, Jagathi Foundation collaborates with corporates, government bodies, institutions, and community leaders to create long-term change at the grassroots level.

Explore our journey, impact, and vision — and join us in shaping a stronger, healthier, and more inclusive nation.

Collaborations with corporations
Supported several of Jagathi Foundation's women's health awareness initiatives, including Project Tejaswini and Project Prerana. These programs focus on topics like cervical cancer awareness, breast cancer awareness, and menstrual hygiene management.
Government of Telangana
Jagathi Foundation has met with and is looking to collaborate with the Government of Telangana on future initiatives. In 2025, they met with Minister Seethakka, who was reportedly impressed by the foundation's projects.
Partnerships with institutions

Engagement with community leaders
Local volunteers: Relies on its large volunteer base to execute projects on the ground, such as the summer camps and community health drives.
